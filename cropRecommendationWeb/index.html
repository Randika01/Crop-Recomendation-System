About the Platform

We aim to empower agricultural officers and farmers across Sri Lanka by leveraging AI technology to make informed, data-driven crop decisions. Our platform addresses the challenges of low productivity and inconsistent crop selection by focusing on the following objectives:

  • AI-Driven Analysis
  • Tailored Crop Recommendations
  • Nationwide Crop Monitoring
